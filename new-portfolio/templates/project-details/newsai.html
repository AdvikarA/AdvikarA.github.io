NewsAI - Reimagining News Consumption

A platform designed to challenge echo chambers and present news from diverse viewpoints, helping users understand different perspectives on current events.

Role: I built it
Personalized News Dashboard
Multi-Perspective News Analysis
AI-Powered News Assistant

Inspiration

Today, the same news event can be covered and presented widely differently. Some of the most pressing challenges today come from our inability to debate, discuss, and reconcile with people from opposing view points. How do we find the information that all the sources agree on? What if we could stay informed on how source bias impacts news. What if we could challenge our own beliefs and avoid echo chambers? What if... News was reimagined?

What it does

NewsAI has three core functionalities:

  • Personalized News Feed: Creates a customized news feed based on a survey that users take, designed to both challenge beliefs and show relevant content
  • Multi-Perspective Analysis: Displays information from different viewpoints, contrasting differences, highlighting similarities, and identifying sources of bias
  • AI News Assistant: Features an embedded chatbot that summarizes the most recent news and answers questions about current events

How we built it

NewsAI takes in user data through a political compass test and measures how likely the user is to engage in disagreement and debate charitably. This data helps classify the user and personalize their newsfeed to challenge their beliefs. For our general news tool which returns similarities and differences, we use NewsAPI, BraveAPI, and Claude to process recent events and analyze.

Challenges we ran into

  • Implementing secure authentication and login systems
  • Efficiently fetching and caching images from various news sources
  • Managing API rate limits across multiple news data providers
  • Integrating Claude AI with Brave and NewsAPI for coherent analysis
